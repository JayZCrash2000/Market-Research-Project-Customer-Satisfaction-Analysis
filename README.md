# 📊 Market Research Project: Customer Satisfaction Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing **customer satisfaction data** for a financial institution offering loan services. The objective is to identify key drivers of satisfaction, regional performance gaps, and actionable insights to improve customer experience.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Measure overall customer satisfaction  
- Analyze satisfaction across regions and customer segments  
- Evaluate Net Promoter Score (NPS)  
- Identify operational gaps and improvement areas  
- Provide data-driven business recommendations  

---

## 📁 Dataset
- Source: Customer satisfaction survey data  
- Includes:
  - Customer ratings across service parameters  
  - Regional segmentation (East, West, North, South)  
  - Loan tenure categories  
  - NPS classification

## 🔍 Key Insights

### ✅ Strengths
- Staff behavior (politeness, knowledge, availability)  
- Fast loan disbursement process  
- Strong performance in South region  

---

### ⚠️ Weaknesses
- Poor post-disbursement communication (welcome letter delay)  
- Regional inconsistency in service quality  
- Weak experience in 1–3 year tenure segment  

---

### 📉 Statistical Findings
- Most metrics statistically significant (p < 0.05)  
- Strong correlation:
  - Loan processing experience ↔ Satisfaction = **0.64**  
- Variance = **2.48** → indicates inconsistent experiences :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 🧭 Regional Strategy
- Replicate South zone best practices  
- Conduct audits in North & West  
- Cross-region training programs  

---

### 📣 Customer Experience
- Automate welcome communication  
- Introduce digital welcome kits  
- Improve clarity in loan explanation  

---

### 🎯 NPS Improvement
- Convert passives into promoters  
- Offer incentives for high ratings  
- Follow-up surveys for neutral customers  

---

### 🧩 Product Strategy
- Simplify process for 1–3 year loans  
- Offer loyalty incentives for short-term customers
